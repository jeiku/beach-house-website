Edisto Beach

Edisto Beach, South Carolina is a beautiful uncommercialized, family-oriented beach! Rich in Native American history, Edisto Beach on Edisto Island is home to one of four oceanfront state parks in South Carolina. Edisto Beach State Park features trails for hiking and biking that provide a wonderful tour of the park. There are so many great things around the Island Manor, come check it out yourself!
